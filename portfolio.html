Research

Assessing Spatial Structure in Marine Populations

In collaboration with Dr. Wendy Gentleman of the Department of Engineering Mathematics and Internetworking at Dalhousie University and Fisheries and Oceans Canada (DFO), I am undertaking a research project concerned with exploring the Python library Infomap (and the potentially complementary Julia program Circuitscape) to determine its suitability for identifying biogeographic boundaries in marine systems by using community detection algorithms.


This research project will last throughout Summer 2021.
